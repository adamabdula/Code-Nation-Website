About me


Hello and thank you for visiting my website. I am recent graduate from the University of Essex with a BSc in Actuarial science. I really enjoyed my course which consisted of financial mathematics, economics, and statistical programming. I really wanted to further develop my programming skills which is why I decided to take part in Code Nations 3-week developer course. The course has given me the basic skills in Python, CSS and HTML. The course really sparked a deep interest in programming, and I am now very eager to keep developing these skills. I will be applying for the Code Nation 12-week course. I have also signed up to free online courses in Python and C to further develop this new passion of mine. I never thought I would be a programmer one day but now I know it is what I want to do for my career. I hope you like my website as it has given me the chance to showcase my CSS and HTML skills.
